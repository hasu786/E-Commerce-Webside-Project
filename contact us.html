Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business days. International shipping may take 10-14 business days depending on the destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached. Returns are free for store credit. For refunds to original payment method, a small restocking fee may apply.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. You can calculate shipping costs at checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.
